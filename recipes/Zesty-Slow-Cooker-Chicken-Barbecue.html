Zesty Slow Cooker Chicken Barbecue

This recipe is very delicious, we highly recommend that you use a slow cooker in order to cook the chicken in just the way we need to.

  • 6 frozen skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (12 ounce) bottle barbecue sauce
  • 1/2 cup italian salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  1. Place chicken in slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix barbecue sauce, italian salad dressing, brown sugar, and Worcestershire Sauce
  3. Cover, then cook 3 to 4 hours on High heat or alternatively 6-8 hours on Low hear
