Old school Pancakes

This recipe of the pancakes will remind you of times when people didn't have supercomputers on the palm of their hands

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Place flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in a large bowl; stir
  2. Make well in the center and pour milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth
  3. Heat lightly oil griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cups for each pancake
  5. When brown on both sides, serve hot
